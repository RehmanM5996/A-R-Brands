Our story

Built by a chemist who was tired of compromise

A-R Cosmetics started in a rented lab bench in 2018, with one brief: formulas that are gentle enough for sensitive skin and pigmented enough to actually work.

How we got here

A slow, deliberate build

2018

The first serum

Founder and cosmetic chemist Elena Marsh formulates the original Velvet Repair Serum for her own rosacea-prone skin.

2020

Shade lab opens

AURELLE builds a 40-shade foundation matching system, tested across a panel of 500 real skin tones — not swatches on paper.

2022

Leaping Bunny certified

Every formula becomes independently certified cruelty-free, with the full ingredient list published on every product page.

2024

Fragrance line launches

AURELLE extends into low-alcohol eau de parfums, formulated for sensitised skin without sacrificing depth.

What we stand for

Three commitments we don't compromise on

Transparency

Full INCI ingredient lists, sourcing notes and lab reports published on every product page.

Inclusivity

Every colour range is matched against a real, diverse panel before it ships — not adjusted after complaints.

Restraint

We'd rather ship twelve formulas we're proud of than fifty that chase a trend cycle.
